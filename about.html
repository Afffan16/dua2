Our Mission


Wardrobe to Create Impressions' is the driving force behind our business. We aim to contribute and make a valuable addition to people's lives by providing a clothing line up of the highest level of elegance.

Our History


Established in 1980, Armoured Officail has been in the fashion wear business for over 40 years now. Armoured Officail is the oldest & absolute menswear brand of Pakistan. Starting as a clothing brand for women and kids; Armoured Offical realized the need for a men's formal wear brand and so in 1991, Armoured Offical launched its formal suiting line up for men. Over the years, Armoured Offical kept growing and launching products as per the contemporary men's fashion. With every passing year the wardrobe for men kept improving due to the untiring efforts of stylist designers and team.
